## 📌 Overview

Urban flooding is one of the most overlooked yet devastating challenges in modern cities. Rapid urbanization, climate change, and poor drainage systems lead to frequent waterlogging, infrastructure damage, and public safety risks.

**Urban Flood Hydrology Engine** is an AI-powered platform that transforms flood management from **reactive response → proactive intelligence**.

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Cities today face:
- 🌧️ Unpredictable rainfall patterns
- 🏙️ Increased concrete surfaces reducing water absorption
- 🚧 Inefficient drainage systems
- ⏱️ Delayed response to flooding events

⚠️ Current systems are mostly **reactive**, leading to:
- Traffic paralysis  
- Economic losses  
- Health hazards  
- Poor resource allocation  

---

## ✅ Our Solution

We built an **end-to-end AI Flood Intelligence Platform** that:

- 📊 Predicts flood risks before they occur  
- 📍 Identifies **micro-level flood hotspots**  
- 🌊 Simulates water flow using hydrology models  
- 🗺️ Visualizes insights on a **real-time GIS dashboard**  
- 👥 Enables **citizen reporting** for ground validation  
- 🚨 Provides actionable alerts & readiness scores  
- ⚙️ Optimizes resource allocation (pumps, manpower, response)

👉 Unlike traditional tools, we combine **AI + Simulation + Data + Decision Support** into one unified system.
