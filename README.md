## 📌 Overview

Urban flooding is one of the most overlooked yet devastating challenges in modern cities. Rapid urbanization, climate change, and poor drainage systems lead to frequent waterlogging, infrastructure damage, and public safety risks.

**Urban Flood Hydrology Engine** is an end-to-end AI platform that combines **Machine Learning + Hydrology Simulation + GIS Visualization + Citizen Intelligence** into one unified decision-support system for smart cities.

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Cities today face:

- 🌧️ Unpredictable rainfall patterns driven by climate change
- 🏙️ Increased concrete surfaces reducing natural water absorption
- 🚧 Inefficient and aging drainage infrastructure
- ⏱️ Delayed, reactive responses to flooding events

**The result:** Traffic paralysis, economic losses, health hazards, and poor resource allocation.

---

## ✅ Our Solution

An **AI Flood Intelligence Platform** that:

- 📊 Predicts flood risks **before** they occur using ML models
- 📍 Identifies **micro-level flood hotspots** at ward level
- 🌊 Simulates water flow using hydrology models
- 🗺️ Visualizes insights on a **real-time GIS dashboard**
- 👥 Enables **citizen reporting** for live ground validation
- 🚨 Provides actionable early alerts for authorities & citizens
- ⚙️ Optimizes deployment of pumps, manpower & emergency services
